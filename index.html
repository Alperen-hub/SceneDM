Abstract

Given the lane graph and historical trajectories as input, SceneDiffuser can effectively capture the inherent multi-modal behavior of vehicles, enabling the simulation of realistic traffic scenarios while adhering to traffic rules. SceneDiffuser considers the temporal consistency and smoothness of future trajectories, thereby enhancing the overall realism of the generated scenes.
